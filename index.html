With a long history of design and aesthetics, Copenhagen has become an scandinavian hub for international fashion. Brands such as Ganni, Moss Copenhagen or NÜ appear everyday in the spotlight of various magazines, channels and social media. Here in Chase, the official website of a personal shopper based in Copenhagen, we explore the world of styling and the opportunities that our own, and the most international fashion have to offer us. Under the slogan ‘Making fashion accessible to anyone’ i help everyone that decides to become my customer to express through their attires, regardless of gender, age , size or economic status.
